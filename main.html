User Guide

CopyKats allows you to compare two text files, and see the level of similarity in percentage and in content.

To use click on "Upload files" button to load your text from your local files. CopyKats allow only the following text file extension: .TXT

About CopyKats

This tool was created to compare students submissions for similarity, ensuring that students do not copy themselves when they submit essays and assignments.
